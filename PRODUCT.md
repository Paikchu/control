# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Active investors using a local research and backtest tool while comparing ETF allocation rules, drawdown triggers, and individual holdings.

## Product Purpose
The product lets the user configure portfolio amounts, convert natural-language strategy notes into editable rules, and inspect backtest outcomes without leaving the workflow.

## Brand Personality
Analytical, direct, controlled. The interface should feel like a serious trading desk utility, not a consumer finance dashboard.

## Anti-references
Avoid marketing-page treatment, pastel SaaS cards, decorative AI badges, vague status copy, and controls that hide the actual trading implication.

## Design Principles
- Put capital, ticker, and rule changes closest to the result they affect.
- Make primary actions visually decisive and secondary actions quiet.
- Keep dense data readable under repeated use.
- Preserve editable strategy text instead of locking the user into one generated answer.
- Use contrast to signal risk, action, and selection.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast. Support keyboard focus, reduced motion, and color-independent state cues.
